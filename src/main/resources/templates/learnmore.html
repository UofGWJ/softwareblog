How to use find Issues

  1. Use the filter tool on the left side of the screen to query.
  2. Click the page change button on the right side of the screen to change the page
  3. Click on the issue you are interested in to enter the details page
  4. The left side of the detail page is the author’s detailed information, click to link to the author’s Github homepage
  5. On the upper centre side of the screen is the detailed information of Issue
  6. The lower part of the screen is the recommended Issue information,Click to link to the Issue details page
  7. On the left side of the screen is the recommended StackOVerflow information,Click to link to Stackoverflow page
  8. You can also record the browsing history after logging in
  9. After logging in, you can add a like button and comment on the function
